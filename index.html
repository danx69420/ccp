Car Club of Pune is an initiative dedicated to building a vibrant community of passionate car collectors and enthusiasts across the city. Our aim is to nurture and celebrate the love for automobiles among Punekars - children and adults alike - creating an environment where families can come together and cherish the memories they create, one mile at a time.

Purpose of the Event

We believe there is hardly a boy, girl, or gentleman in this world who isn't fascinated by power - whether it comes from the roar of an engine or the might of a military machine. Even someone driving a Ferrari would feel goosebumps standing beside a ground-shaking Indian Army tank or witnessing the strength and discipline of our soldiers up close.

For every gearhead and adrenaline enthusiast, our purpose is simple yet meaningful: to give the youth a chance to experience their dreams firsthand — to stand beside the heroes they admire, witness the machines they've only seen from afar, and ignite within them a sense of passion, responsibility, and purpose.

Through this show, we aim to motivate the next generation to chase their dreams with determination and integrity, inspired by the courage and commitment exemplified by the Indian Army.
